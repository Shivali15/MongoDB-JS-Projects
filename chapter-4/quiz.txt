Chapter 4: Resiliency
Connection Pooling
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q1.Which of the following are benefits of connection pooling?

ANS:
A large influx of operations can be handled more quickly with a pool of existing connections.
New operations can be serviced with pre-existing connections, so a new connection doesn't have 
to be created each time.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When our application is using a Write Concern more durable than w: 1.
